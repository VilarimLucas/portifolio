Micro Certification in Basic Front-End

Micro Certification consists of a digital credential or medal, also known in the market as a badge, which indicates that the student has acquired a quality or skill in a specific area of knowledge or technology, provided for in the training itinerary of their course, in order to connect their technological training with the world of work.

Once the Higher Technology Course is structured by skills and with training itineraries, the Faculty of Technology - Fatec at Centro Paula Souza validates the student's compliance with the curricular components and issues Micro Certifications.
