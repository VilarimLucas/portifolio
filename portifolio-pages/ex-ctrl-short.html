Expense Control App

EX Ctrl is a cross-platform personal finance system, where it is possible to register expenses and income so that recommendation algorithms can calculate the user's Cost of Living and recommend extra investments and income using task lists. The project was approved in the 1st phase of FETEPS, check out the pitch on YouTube by clicking here.
