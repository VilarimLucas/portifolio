Predictive Maintenance Management System

This project emerged during the beginning of the event at Fatec Cotia. Predictive Maintenance is a topic little explored among innovation projects for CPTM, the purpose of the challenge is to create a solution that covers all of CPTM's internal resources.

GMP (Gestão de Manutenção Preditiva) stands for Predictive Maintenance Management in Portuguese-Brazil
